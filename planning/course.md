# Course Information

## Course

- **Course:** [MIT 1.001: Engineering Computation and Data Science](https://student.mit.edu/catalog/search.cgi?search=1.001)
- **Description:** Introduces the fundamentals of computational thinking and data science in engineering computation.
- **Term:** Spring 2026
- **Instructors:** [Abel Sanchez](https://abel.mit.edu/) and [John R. Williams](https://johntango.github.io/)
- **Website:** [onexi.org](https://onexi.org)

## Grading Rubric

| Criteria | Points | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Complexity and Sophistication | 15 | Design, coding, and user experience should show appropriate complexity. Neither too simplistic nor too complex. Should be interesting. |
| User Interface | 15 | Polished UI appropriate for the project. |
| Effort | 15 | Should clearly reflect the quantity and quality of design and craftsmanship. |
| Design | 15 | Well-chosen functions, data structures, and algorithms. |
| Video and Presentation | 40 | Clearly highlight the most important and interesting aspects. Voiceover should be clear, concise, and easy to understand. |
| Bonus | 0--10+ | Extraordinary work. Discuss ambitious attempts with instructor first. |
